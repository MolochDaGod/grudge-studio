📖 System Overview

This document details how the 8 game attributes impact player stats. Each attribute point invested provides both flat bonuses (fixed amounts) and percentage bonuses (scaling with base stats).

✅ System Status: All critical issues have been resolved. The attribute system now features balanced scaling, diminishing returns after 25 points, and comprehensive stat caps to prevent overflow.

Core Concepts

  • Flat Bonuses: Fixed amount added per attribute point (e.g., +26 Health per Strength)
  • Percentage Bonuses: Scales with base stats from level (e.g., +0.8% of base Health per Strength)
  • Diminishing Returns: Reduced efficiency after 25 points in any attribute
  • Stat Caps: Maximum values for combat stats to maintain balance
Total Stat = (Flat Bonus × Points) + (Base Stat × Percent Bonus × Points)

🧮 Combat Math & Damage Calculations

Health Calculation

Max Health = Base Health + Attribute Bonuses + Equipment + Traits + Sets

Attribute Bonus = Σ(Flat × Points) + (Base × Σ(Percent × Points))

Example: Level 10, Strength 10, Vitality 5

Base Health: 100
Strength: (26×10) + (100 × 0.008×10) = 260 + 8 = 268
Vitality: (25×5) + (100 × 0.005×5) = 125 + 2.5 = 127.5
Total: 100 + 268 + 127.5 = 495.5 HP

Mana Calculation

Same formula applies for Mana resource pool

Example: Intellect 10, Wisdom 5

Base Mana: 50
Intellect: (5×10) + (50 × 0.05×10) = 50 + 25 = 75
Wisdom: (20×5) + (50 × 0.03×5) = 100 + 7.5 = 107.5
Total: 50 + 75 + 107.5 = 232.5 Mana

Damage Output Formula

Base Damage = Level Damage + Attribute Bonuses + Equipment + Traits + Sets

Defense & Damage Reduction (Relational Damage)

Damage Taken = Incoming Damage × (100 - √Defense) / 100
Defense Square Root Damage Reduction Example (100 Damage)
100 10 10% 90 damage
400 20 20% 80 damage
900 30 30% 70 damage
2500 50 50% 50 damage

Block System

IF Random(0,1) < Block Chance:
  Damage = Damage × (1 - Block Factor)

Block Chance Cap: 75%
Block Factor Cap: 90% (0.90)

Example: 100 damage incoming, 30% block chance, 0.3 block factor

  • 30% chance to trigger block
  • If blocked: 100 × (1 - 0.3) = 70 damage taken
  • Modified by attacker's Block Break Factor

Critical Hit System

IF Random(0,1) < Critical Chance:
  Damage = Damage × Critical Factor

Critical Chance Cap: 75%
Critical Factor Cap: 3.0× (300% damage)

Example: 100 base damage, 25% crit chance, 1.5× crit factor

  • 25% chance to trigger critical
  • If critical: 100 × 1.5 = 150 damage
  • Modified by target's Critical Evasion

Full Combat Calculation Pipeline

  1. Calculate Base Damage - Level + Attributes + Equipment + Traits
  2. Apply Defense Break - Reduce target defense by attacker's Defense Break Factor
  3. Calculate Mitigation - Apply defense formula (relational or flat)
  4. Apply Random Variance - ±25% deviation (if enabled)
  5. Check Block - Roll vs effective block chance (modified by Block Break)
  6. Check Critical - Roll vs effective crit chance (modified by Crit Evasion) if not blocked
  7. Apply Damage - Subtract from target HP
  8. Trigger Effects - Drain, Reflect, Absorb factors activate
Note: Critical hits cannot occur on blocked attacks. Block check happens before critical check.

Drain Mechanics (Lifesteal/Manasteal)

Health Restored = Damage Dealt × Drain Health Factor
Mana Restored = Damage Dealt × Drain Mana Factor

Cap: 50% (0.50)

Reflect Damage

Damage Reflected = Damage Received × Reflect Factor
Cap: 50% (0.50)

Reflect does NOT proc on blocked hits

Absorb Mechanics

Health Regenerated = Damage Received × Absorb Health Factor
Mana Regenerated = Damage Received × Absorb Mana Factor

Cap: 50% (0.50)

Accuracy & Resistance (Debuffs)

Debuff Success = Attacker Accuracy - Target Resistance

Cap: 95% (0.95) - Always 5% minimum chance to resist/land

The Problem

Many attributes had negative percentage values (-39.1%, -79.2%, -66.1%, etc.) that caused stats to decrease catastrophically at high levels.

Example of broken scaling:

Strength with 50 points (BEFORE FIX):
Damage = (3 × 50) + (BaseDamage × -0.391 × 50)
       = 150 + (50 × -19.55)
       = 150 - 977.5
       = -827.5 DAMAGE (BROKEN!)

The Solution

Changed all negative percentages to small positive values:

Attribute Stat Before (Broken) After (Fixed)
Strength Damage % -39.1% +2%
Strength Defense % -79.2% +1.5%
Dexterity Critical % -66.1% +1.2%
Agility Defense % -44.4% +0.8%
Tactics Block % -65.4% +0.8%
Result: All attributes now provide positive bonuses that scale correctly at ALL levels (1-100+ points)
