Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 14, 2026

Grudge Studio ("we", "us", "our") operates grudgewarlords.com and related services including warlord-crafting-suite.vercel.app, api.grudge-studio.com, id.grudge-studio.com, gdevelop-assistant.vercel.app, dungeon-crawler-quest.vercel.app, and associated game clients (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information. When you create a Grudge ID you may provide:

  • Username
  • Email address (optional)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Password (stored as a salted hash — we never store plaintext passwords)

Third-Party Authentication. If you sign in via Discord, Google, GitHub, Phantom wallet, or Puter, we receive:

  • Your public profile information (display name, avatar, unique ID) from the provider
  • Wallet public address (Phantom/Solana only — we never access your private keys)
  • Puter cloud UUID and username

Gameplay Data. We collect game session data including character stats, inventory, crafting progress, faction membership, match history, and in-game chat messages.

Device & Usage Data. We automatically collect IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and timestamps. Guest users are assigned a random device ID stored in your browser.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Grudge ID account
  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • To enable multiplayer gameplay, matchmaking, and leaderboards
  • To sync game progress across devices via Puter cloud storage
  • To communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support
  • To detect and prevent abuse, cheating, and unauthorized access
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secured servers hosted via Railway (backend services) and Vercel (frontend applications). We use PostgreSQL and Redis databases with encrypted connections (TLS/SSL). Authentication tokens are signed with JWT and transmitted over HTTPS only.

We implement industry-standard security measures including Helmet.js security headers, CORS restrictions, rate limiting, and input validation. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Discord — OAuth authentication (Discord Privacy Policy)
  • Google — OAuth authentication (Google Privacy Policy)
  • GitHub — OAuth authentication (GitHub Privacy Statement)
  • Phantom — Solana wallet connection (Phantom Privacy Policy)
  • Puter — Cloud storage and authentication (Puter Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel — Frontend hosting
  • Railway — Backend hosting
  • Cloudflare — DNS and CDN

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage (not traditional cookies) to store:

  • Authentication tokens (JWT)
  • Your Grudge ID and username
  • Guest device ID
  • Puter guest ID for cloud sync
  • UI preferences (e.g. saved links on the Nexus hub)

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • Public gameplay data: Usernames, levels, factions, and match results are visible to other players
  • Service providers: Hosting and infrastructure providers that process data on our behalf
  • Legal compliance: When required by law, court order, or to protect safety

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data via your account profile
  • Update your account information at any time
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing
  • Export your data upon request

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
